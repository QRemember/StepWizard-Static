In Loving Memory

On Saturday, May 28th, 2022, Adrianus (Ed) Lugtenburg was called home at the age of 83. After several months of declining health, Adrianus died peacefully at home in Whitby, Ontario with his beloved wife Margaret, their children, and family by his side. Adrianus was born in Spijkenisse, Netherlands, on January 6th, 1939. He relocated to Canada as a teenager with his father and mother and 4 younger brothers.
