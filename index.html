Welcome to the QRemember contribution page

Dear Ron, Thank you for starting the quick and easy process of contributing to Peggy Velasquez’s QR Memorial. We here at QRemember hope you find this process to be simple and rewarding. Below we lay out the process which allows for two different types of contributions: 1.) Photos with the option for captions 2.) Stories about Peggy Velasquez.

See How It Works

Photos with the option for captions:

  • First, you will be asked to click the “upload” button below to begin uploading photos of Peggy Velasquez from your device’s camera roll.
  • After uploading the photos you wish to contribute, you will have an opportunity to add captions to those photos.
  • The captions are meant to discuss the memory associated with the photo you contributed.
  • If you don’t wish to contribute a memory in this way but would rather just offer stories, click the skip button!

Stories about Peggy Velasquez:

  • After you have uploaded your photos and caption, you will be taken to a second page which gives you an opportunity to write out fond memories shared with Peggy Velasquez.
  • If you don’t wish to contribute a memory in this way, just click finish!
